Stars FAQs

What is GetCeleb?

GetCeleb is a website and app where fans can buy personalized video shoutouts for special occasions and everyday moments from their favorite stars. It’s an easy way for you to connect with fans, make people incredibly happy, and earn money or fundraise for a cause you’re passionate about.

How does it work?

On the GetCeleb app, a purchaser can select a star and request a personalized shoutout, specifying the name of the receiver and the occasion. If the star accepts, he/she will create and send a one-of-a-kind video back to the purchaser for sharing.

How do I get started?

Just visit GetCeleb.in or download the app. We will help you create your celebrity account. It’s that easy. You’ll then set price you want to charge for each video which you can always change at any point in time.

Once your account has been created, we’ll inform you and your profile will be available for fan requests.

Does it cost anything to sign up?

No, signing up is free. We only get paid when you get paid.

Do I have any obligations if I sign up for GetCeleb?

There are no obligations except for completing videos that your fans have bought from you.

Can I decline a request?

Absolutely. If you get a request that you’re uncomfortable with, you can simply decline it in the app. We track consumers with declined requests and will block individuals from making future requests if abuse persists.

How quickly am I expected to fulfil a request?

Sooner the better of course, but we request 1-2 days on average. You have 7 days to fulfil the request before the request expires and the fan gets refunded.

What if I’m unable to take requests?

In such cases just let us know at marketing@getceleb.in and we will make your profile unavailable for temporary basis.

Are there any rules for GetCeleb videos?

Consumers cannot request (and you cannot create) violent, nude, partially nude, discriminatory, unlawful, infringing, hateful, pornographic, or sexually suggestive videos or other content via GetCeleb.

How do I set the price for my videos?

You set a fixed price for a video. You can change this at any time by going to account section of your app.

Over time, our pricing engine will maximize your earnings — for example, we’ll help you to raise your price when demand increases.

Can I do GetCeleb videos for charity?

Contributing to a good cause is always rewarding. On your profile, you may state that you are donating part or all of your proceeds to a specific charity. It will be up to you to ensure that the proceeds are sent to that charity

How long is a typical GetCeleb video?

It’s up to you. We request a minimum of 15 seconds to avoid disappointing your fans, but expect some stars will create much longer videos as they see fit.

Will fans see my personal contact information if I do a video?

No. GetCeleb does not make your personal contact information available to the consumer.

How do I get paid?

GetCeleb will typically release payouts of your earnings into your specified bank account once every week. You can always look at the number of videos you have done, amount disbursed and other stats in your account section.

Can my fans from outside India order GetCeleb videos?

Absolutely. As long as they have an accepted payment method they can order a video.
